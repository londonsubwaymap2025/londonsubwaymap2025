2. How do I interpret the contour lines on a 6 inch to 1 mile map?

Contour lines connect points of equal elevation. The closer the contour lines are to each other, the steeper the slope. Conversely, widely spaced contour lines indicate a gentle slope.

3. What are the limitations of a 6 inch to 1 mile map?
